Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope, the start of everything star wars. Without this movie a lot of people wouldn't be who they are today including myself, a giant nerd.

My favorite actor from this movie is Mark Hamill. He brings charisma to Luke and fits the role perfectly by portraying an eager teenager ready to explore the galaxy.
