Review

Nathan Bergeron October 21, 2025

Star Wars: Episode VI - Return of the Jedi, is in my personal opinion the best movie in the original trilogy. The story is all tied together and the conflict resolution is satisfying and not rushed. The worlds are interesting and there is a steady pace in the story.

My favorite actor in this movie is Mark Hamill. Continuing his legacy of playing Luke Skywalker he's visually grown to understand the character inside and out and does not disappoint in his acting.


A few fun facts

  • Jabba the Hutt was a giant animatronic operated by 6 people

  • Endor was originally supposed to be inhabited by wookies

  • Yoda was originally not supposed to be in the movie
