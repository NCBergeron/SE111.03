STAR WARS

A Long Time Ago in a Galaxy Far Far Away . . . .


The core foundation of the Star Wars story is the Skywalker Saga, a series of nine episodic films released with the original Star Wars film in 1977 and ending with the ninth film in 2019. The saga follows three generations of the Skywalker family in three trilogies across several galactic conflicts. Beyond this, Star Wars has expanded through other media, with the Star Wars Legends continuity comprising of media beyond the Skywalker saga prior to the April 2014 continuity reset, and new expanded universe stories falling under one unified canon. In recent years, the Star Wars franchise has significantly expanded with the development and release of spin-off theatrical films, new television series, and large-scale publishing initiatives.
