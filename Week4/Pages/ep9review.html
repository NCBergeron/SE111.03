Review

Nathan Bergeron October 21, 2025

Star Wars: Episode IX - The Rise of Skywalker, is in my opinion the best star wars movie out of the sequels. I like it because I recognize it as an attempt to salvage the writing nightmare that the sequels are. Ignoring the writing, the visuals are good and it can be an enjoyable watch.

My favorite actor in this movie is Adam Driver. He did a nice job portraying someone coming back to the light after being in the dark for a long time.

A few fun facts

  • Denis Lawson reprised his role as Wedge Antilles after turning the opportunity down for the force awakens after saying it would be boring

  • Ed Sheeran and John Williams were stormtroopers among other celebrities

  • Babu Frik (the really tiny droid mechanic) was a real nine inch tall puppet that needed seven people to operate
