Review

Nathan Bergeron October 21, 2025

Star Wars: Episode VII - The Force Awakens, is a mixed bag of opinions. It should have been a bit more unique like the other movies insteadly of having an uncanny amount of parallels to A New Hope. The cinematography and effects were great but the writing lacked originality.

My favorite actor in this movie was Oscar Isaac who played the pilot Poe Dameron. Oscar made Poe feel like a real person who people could relate to and who wasn't an overly gifted person.
