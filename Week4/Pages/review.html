Review

Nathan Bergeron October 21, 2025

Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith, is in my opinion the best star wars movie ever made. it has the best choreography and cinematics of any of the movies with the most impactful moments in the star wars universe.

My favorite actor from this movie is Hayden Christensen. He is a very polite person in real life and he portrayed anakins internal conflict incredibly well in the movies. He is in my opinion the best actor for Anakin just as Ewan McGregor was the best actor for Obi-Wan.
