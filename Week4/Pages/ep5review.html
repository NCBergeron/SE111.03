Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back, is regarded as the most popular of the original trilogy and I believe this is because this is because this is the movie where we can see the universe of star wars really expand and become what it is today.

My favorite actor in this movie is Harrison Ford, his line delivery is deliberate and adds a certain flair that only he could bring to the character Han Solo.

A few fun facts

  • The opening scene where Luke is walking through the snow was shot from their hotel room during a snowstorm

  • The rebel soldiers were played by local mountain rescuers

  • A full sized Millenium Falcon was built as a set
