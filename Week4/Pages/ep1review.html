Star Wars: Episode I - The Phantom Menace, is a critical movie in setting up characters who's impact will be felt over the next eight movies. it gives insight into how the politics of the era led into the start of the clone wars.

My favorite actor from this movie is Ewan McGregor. He plays an extremely important role in star wars and he does it well.
