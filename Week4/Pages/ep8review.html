Review

Nathan Bergeron October 21, 2025

Star Wars: Episode VIII - The Last Jedi, is in my opinion the worst star wars movie out of any of them. There was a whole lot of nothing going on all in one place the whole movie. The writers for this movie seemed to throw away any story setup from The Force Awakens and start over with their own ideas.

My favorite actor from this movie is probably John Boyega. We got to spend a lot more time with Finn in the movie and get to appreciate his character a little more.


A few fun facts

  • Mark Hamill did hidden cameos in the movie
  • This is the longest star wars movie
  • This movie ended up pulling in $1.333 Billion
